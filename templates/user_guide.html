Printing Guidelines

Use the right paper size

The PDF files are made to be printed on the US Letter or the A4.

Print at a 100% scale

Print at a 100% scale on center, or with the Actual Size option turned on. Do not select Scale to Fit Media or Fit to Printable Area.

Check the scale squares

Ensure the scale squares are printed to the correct sizes -- 1" and 1cm.
